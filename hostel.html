HOSTEL RULES

Occupants must check-in and check-out of the hostel according to the designated times. Late check-outs may be subject to an additional fee.
Only registered occupants are allowed to stay in the hostel. The maximum number of occupants per room is two.
Smoking and alcohol are strictly prohibited within the hostel premises. Violators may be subject to immediate eviction.
Guests are only allowed in designated areas of the hostel and must be registered at the front desk. The maximum number of guests per room is two.
Occupants must store their valuables in the designated lockers or safe boxes. The hostel is not responsible for any lost or stolen items.
Occupants are expected to keep their living spaces clean and tidy at all times. They are also required to participate in cleaning schedules for common areas.
Occupants must comply with all local laws and regulations, including those related to noise, smoking, and alcohol.
Violations of the hostel's rules and regulations may result in penalties, such as fines or eviction.
